# Session 3 visual contract

Session 3 may improve commercial routing, but it must remain visibly part of the existing Gurjas website.

## Reference characteristics

- Deep navy hero and navigation with restrained gold accents.
- Editorial Fraunces headings, Newsreader supporting copy, Archivo interface text and mono metadata.
- Warm cream and subtle tinted section alternation.
- Thin rules, compact labels and generous whitespace.
- Square or two-pixel corners for interface elements; no soft SaaS card language.
- Motion is subtle, optional and removed under `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## Prohibited regressions

- No second stylesheet or independent offer-page theme.
- No excessive gradients, glass panels, floating rounded cards or unrelated icon language.
- No duplicated homepage offer section.
- No material change to the hero, proof ledger, tools, credentials, research library or ethics sections.
- No horizontal overflow at 360px or wider.

## Approval evidence

Before merge, compare the homepage, Services page and every offer page at desktop and mobile widths. Record:

1. No material change to existing sections outside the approved routing edits.
2. Shared header, footer, type, colour and spacing remain consistent.
3. Each offer contains fit, non-fit, deliverables, inputs, method, investment, boundaries, proof, FAQ and one primary CTA.
4. Keyboard focus, reduced motion and local links pass.
