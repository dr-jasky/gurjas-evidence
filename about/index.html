About Gurjas

A research-and-policy practice built around credibility, discretion and usable evidence.

Gurjas Evidence and Policy Analytics is a women-led micro enterprise established to offer serious research support without compromising academic integrity.

Who we are

Gurjas combines social-science depth, advanced quantitative methods, policy relevance and institutional documentation into clear, decision-ready outputs. We were founded on a simple observation: institutions and researchers across India and beyond need analytical support that meets international academic standards, delivered with the discretion and accountability of a professional practice.

Our work is designed for universities, colleges, NGOs, CSR teams, researchers, policy actors and development organisations that need credible analysis, defensible documentation and publication-oriented research guidance. Every engagement is anchored in the team's own peer-reviewed publication record — in journals indexed in Scopus, Web of Science and the ABDC list — so that the standards we advise on are standards we have met ourselves.

Mission

To deliver rigorous, ethical, evidence-based research that helps institutions, scholars and policy actors make better decisions — converting data, field experience and scholarship into outputs that withstand academic, policy and stakeholder scrutiny.

Vision

To become a globally respected, independent evidence and policy organisation: small in footprint, international in standard, and trusted for the integrity of its methods as much as the clarity of its findings.

Core values

  • Evidence before opinion. Claims are grounded in data, method and citation — or they are not made.
  • Integrity without exception. No ghostwriting, no fabricated results, no guaranteed outcomes. Our governance framework is public.
  • Methodological transparency. Analytical choices are documented and defensible at every step.
  • Discretion. Client data and institutional materials are treated as confidential by default.
  • Capability building. We prefer to leave clients more capable than we found them — through mentoring, documentation and reproducible workflows.

How we work

Engagements begin with a clearly framed research question and end with a decision-ready output. In between, we apply a documented pipeline — design, evidence, analysis, decision — described on our methodology page. We work remotely with clients across India, Canada and globally, and we describe client-specific engagements publicly only with prior consent.
