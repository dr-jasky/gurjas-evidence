How to read this. A fee record is neither an endorsement nor a warning by itself. Confirm the current amount, currency, waiver rules, payee and payment timing on the genuine journal website. OpenAlex fields can be incomplete, converted or out of date.

Method and limitations

Method version 1.1 · reviewed 20 July 2026. This page queries OpenAlex source records and reports selected fields without independently checking the journal website or an invoice. The “in DOAJ” label is an OpenAlex-recorded field; use the direct DOAJ link to verify it.

Not checked: quoted invoice amount, journal domain, payee, bank details, acceptance state, waiver eligibility or the current publisher price. Do not make a payment decision from this lookup alone.
