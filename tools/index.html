Research Tools

Transparent research tools for consequential decisions

Free to use, no sign-up required, with named sources and limitations on every result. Six tools process inputs in your browser. The journal shortlist, Journal Evidence Checker, Recorded APC Lookup and Reference & DOI Integrity Checker send only the search term, ISSN, DOI or reference text to the named public databases when you press their action button; Gurjas does not receive those queries.

Choose by task

Ten tools, three research decisions

Start with the decision in front of you: design the study, publish safely, or strengthen institutional readiness.

Plan & validate 4 Publish safely 4 Institutions & funding 2
01

Doctoral planning & research design

Triage the evidence controls, sequence the work, justify the sample and inspect measurement quality before interpreting results.

Start here · Triage

Research Readiness Triage

Turn eight self-reported evidence-control states into a gap register and next-control plan—without an invented readiness score.

Plan

PhD Timeline Planner

Registration date in, milestone plan out — aligned to the UGC PhD Regulations 2022 timeframes, printable, with the honest sequencing most scholars discover too late.

Design

SEM Planning Bounds Explorer

Compare three disclosed planning approximations—and see why none is automatically a model-specific SEM power analysis.

Validate

Reliability & Convergent Validity Calculator

Calculate CR, AVE and Cronbach's alpha with explicit data checks, assumptions and non-universal reference markers.

02

Journal selection & publication integrity

Find candidate journals, verify identity and indexing, check your reference list's registry record, and assess a proposed article-processing charge before submitting or paying.

Most used · Verify

Journal Evidence Checker

Assemble source-labelled identity, registration, indexing-snapshot and clone-alert signals without an unsupported legitimacy score.

Beta · Discover

Literature-neighbour Journal Shortlist

Find related journal-type, ISSN-bearing OpenAlex sources as a shortlist to investigate—not an endorsement or submission recommendation.

New · Verify citations

Reference & DOI Integrity Checker

Check each reference's DOI against Crossref — title, author and year comparison, plus any recorded retraction or correction.

Inspect cost

Recorded APC Lookup

Inspect APC and open-access fields recorded in OpenAlex without treating missing data or directory status as payment advice.

03

Institutional readiness & research funding

Turn broad requirements into a documented readiness conversation and keep official funding calls visible.

Legacy RAF · Readiness

Institutional Evidence Readiness

Review present, partial and missing records across the legacy seven-criteria structure—without generating an unofficial NAAC score.

Funding

Grant Deadline Tracker

ICSSR, ANRF and allied funding calls for Indian researchers on one page, reviewed monthly, with every row linked to its official source.

These tools support planning and evidence review; they do not replace study-specific methodological judgement, current official requirements or formal advice. Inspect every tool's processing, outbound requests, sources and decision boundary in the public tool-contract registry. Suggestions welcome at support@gurjas.org.
