Research Tools

Free, citation-ready instruments for serious researchers

Built by the Gurjas team on the methods we publish with. Free to use, no sign-up required, each grounded in the peer-reviewed literature it cites.

Live

SEM Sample Size Calculator

A-priori sample size for structural equation modeling: Westland's lower bound, Fisher-z power analysis and N:q heuristics — with a copy-ready, fully cited justification paragraph for your methods chapter.

In development

Predatory Journal Risk Checker

Paste a journal name or ISSN and receive a structured, verifiable risk assessment against indexing databases and documented red-flag signals.

In development

NAAC Readiness Scorecard

A twenty-question self-assessment for IQAC coordinators, mapped to the NAAC criteria, with an instant gap summary.

These tools are provided for general research guidance and do not replace methodological judgement or formal advice. Suggestions for new tools are welcome at gurjasevidence@gmail.com.
