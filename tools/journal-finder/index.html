Read this before you rely on the list. Results come live from the open OpenAlex database and reflect published output, which means very new or niche journals may be under-represented and a journal appearing here is not vouched for. Treat this as a starting shortlist: check each candidate's scope against your paper, and verify its legitimacy and indexing with our Predatory Journal Risk Checker before submitting.
