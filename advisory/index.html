Honorary Advisory Board

Eminent advisors, honorary by design.

Gurjas is assembling an honorary, non-executive advisory board to strengthen research quality, international orientation, academic governance, policy relevance and institutional capacity-building. The first advisors are joining; further names will be published as written consents are finalised.

Advisors serve in an honorary, non-executive capacity. Institutional affiliations are stated for identification only and do not imply endorsement by the affiliated institution.

Mandate

The advisory board exists to hold Gurjas to a higher standard than any client could demand: reviewing our methodological practice, advising on research direction, strengthening international orientation and reinforcing the governance commitments published in our ethics framework. Advisors act in a purely honorary, non-executive capacity and carry no ownership, liability or operational role.

Compliance-safe by design

  • Honorary and non-executive — no remuneration-based obligations and no management authority.
  • No institutional endorsement implied — affiliations are stated for identification only.
  • Prior written consent — advisor names, titles and wording appear publicly only after written approval.
  • Conflict-of-interest screening consistent with our COI policy.

Composition

The board is being assembled to span methodology, public policy, higher-education governance, innovation ecosystems and development practice, with a deliberate mix of Indian and international perspectives. Further names and profiles will be published above as consents are finalised.

Expressions of interest

Senior academics and institutional leaders interested in serving in an honorary advisory capacity may write to gurjasevidence@gmail.com.
