What to include in your first note

  • Objective — the decision, publication or institutional requirement the work must serve.
  • Data status — whether data exists, is being collected, or needs to be designed from scratch.
  • Timeline — real deadlines, including submission or accreditation dates.
  • Expected output — report, analysis, brief, documentation, mentoring or a combination.

We typically respond within two working days. Engagements begin only after a written scope is agreed, and all correspondence is treated as confidential under our data protection statement.
