How we work

From research problem to policy-facing evidence.

1Weeks 1–2

Discovery & Scoping

We map your research problem, assess data readiness and define exact methodological requirements — with a transparent scope of work.

2Weeks 2–4

Methodological Design

The architecture of the study — frameworks, evaluation logic, field instruments and statistical models — before any execution begins.

3Timeline varies

Execution & Analytics

Rigorous processing, quantitative analysis, qualitative synthesis and triangulation — peer-review standards throughout.

4Final phase

Evidence Delivery

Decision-ready reporting — a NAAC dossier, an impact evaluation or a defence strategy — with limitations made clear.
